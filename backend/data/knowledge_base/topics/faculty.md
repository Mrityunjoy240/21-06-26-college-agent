## Teaching Quality

**Approach**: Student-centric with focus on practical applications

**Methods**:

- Interactive classroom teaching
- Lab sessions with hands-on practice
- Industry projects and case studies
- Guest lectures from industry experts
- Regular assessments and feedback

**Evaluation**: Continuous evaluation through assignments, presentations, and exams

**Support**: Mentor system for each student batch, doubt-clearing sessions, and extra classes for weak students

**Faculty Development**: Regular faculty training programs and workshops to stay updated with industry trends
