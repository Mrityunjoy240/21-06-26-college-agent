# Anti-Ragging Policy

BCREC has a **Zero Tolerance** policy against ragging.

## Measures

- Dedicated Anti-Ragging Committee
- Active Anti-Ragging Squad that conducts surprise raids
- Legal affidavit signed by both students and parents at admission
- Separate fresher hostels for safety
- Regular monitoring and surprise checks
