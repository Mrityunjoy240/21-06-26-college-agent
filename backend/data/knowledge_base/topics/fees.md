## Payment Modes

Accepted: Demand Draft, Debit Card, Credit Card
Not accepted: Cheque, Cash

## Hidden Charges

No hidden charges. All fees are transparently listed in the official fee brochure.

## Refund Policy

- **Guidelines**: As per AICTE/UGC guidelines
- **Within timeline**: Full refund minus Rs. 1,000 processing fee, prospectus fee, and dress kit charges (if already made)
- **Post-timeline (with replacement)**: Refund minus processing fee and proportionate tuition/hostel charges if replacement student fills the seat
- **Post-timeline (no replacement)**: Only caution deposit is refundable; all other academic and admission fees are forfeited

## Installments

Semester-wise payment. Contact college for hardship cases.
