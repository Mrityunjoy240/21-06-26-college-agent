# BCREC Fee Structure (2026-30)

## B.Tech Fees

### Branch-wise Total Fees (4 years)

| Branch Group | Total Fee | Admission Fee | Per Semester |
|-------------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| CSE, IT, ECE | ₹6,04,700 | ₹98,225 | ₹72,925 |
| EE, AIML, DS, CY, CSD | ₹5,54,100 | ₹91,900 | ₹66,100 |
| Mechanical, Civil | ₹4,44,100 | ₹78,150 | ₹40,800 |

### Semester-wise Breakdown (CSE/IT/ECE)
- **First Semester**: ₹98,225 (includes admission fee, caution deposit, library fee, and tuition)
- **Semesters 2-7**: ₹72,925 each
- **Eighth Semester**: ₹73,925
- **Total**: ₹6,09,700 (lumpsum) or ₹6,04,700 (installment)

### Semester-wise Breakdown (EE/AIML/DS/CY/CSD)
- **First Semester**: ₹91,900
- **Semesters 2-7**: ₹66,100 each
- **Eighth Semester**: ₹67,100
- **Total**: ₹5,54,100

### Semester-wise Breakdown (ME/CE)
- **First Semester**: ₹78,150
- **Semesters 2-7**: ₹40,800 each
- **Eighth Semester**: ₹41,800
- **Total**: ₹4,44,100

### Payment Information
- **Mode**: Semester-wise payment
- **Methods**: Demand Draft, Debit Card, Credit Card
- **Not Accepted**: Cheque, Cash
- **No hidden charges** — all fees are transparently listed

## Scholarships

1. **Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW)**: 5% seats via WBJEE counseling
2. **Swami Vivekananda Merit-cum-Means Scholarship**: ₹60,000/year
3. **Kanyashree K3**: For female students

## Refund Policy
- As per AICTE/UGC guidelines
- Within timeline: Full refund minus ₹1,000 processing fee
- Post-timeline with replacement: Refund minus proportionate charges
- Post-timeline without replacement: Only caution deposit refundable
