# Admission at BCREC

## Eligibility

- **B.Tech**: 10+2 with PCM, minimum 50% marks for general category
- **Entrance Exams**: WBJEE or JEE Main

## Seat Distribution

| Source | Percentage |
|--------|-----------|
| WBJEE | 80% |
| JEE Main | 10% |
| Management Quota | 10% |

- **Lateral Entry**: 10% of approved intake via JELET
- **Spot Round**: Mid-September to early October
- **Counseling**: WBJEEB at wbjeeb.nic.in
- **Application Portal**: https://bcrec.ucanapply.com

## Age Limit

There is no upper age limit. Candidates must be at least 17 years old by December 31st of the admission year as per WBJEEB norms.

## NRI Quota

- **Seats**: 5% of total intake
- **Fee Range**: $3000-5000 per year
- **Contact**: +91-6297128554
