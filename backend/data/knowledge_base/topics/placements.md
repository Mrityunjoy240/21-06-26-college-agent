## Placement Process

1. **Pre-Placement Talk**: Companies visit campus for presentations
2. **Application**: Students apply for eligible companies
3. **Shortlisting**: Based on resume, marks, and criteria
4. **Written Test**: Aptitude and Technical tests
5. **Group Discussion**: For select companies
6. **Personal Interview**: Technical and HR rounds
7. **Result**: Selected students receive offer letters
