# Academic Departments and Programs Overview

When someone asks about "Departments" or "Admission Options", they are referring to the major programs offered at BCREC. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines.

## 1. B.Tech (Undergraduate Engineering Departments)
BCREC offers B.Tech degrees in 10 engineering disciplines. The core NBA-Accredited departments are:
- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) - NBA Accredited
- Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) - NBA Accredited
- Information Technology (IT) - NBA Accredited
- Electrical Engineering (EE) - NBA Accredited
- Mechanical Engineering (ME) - NBA Accredited

Other B.Tech engineering disciplines include CSE-AIML (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning), CSE-Data Science, CSE-Cyber Security, Computer Science and Design (CSD), and Civil Engineering (CE).

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT: First-Year Subjects vs. Admission Departments
Mathematics (Math), Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (Bio) are **foundational first-year subjects** — they are taught to all engineering students in the first year. They are **NOT separate B.Tech branches** and you **CANNOT take admission** into them as degree programs.

The actual B.Tech admission departments at BCREC are:
1. Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) — কম্পিউটার সায়েন্স অ্যান্ড ইঞ্জিনিয়ারিং
2. Information Technology (IT) — ইনফরমেশন টেকনোলজি
3. Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) — ইলেকট্রনিক্স অ্যান্ড কমিউনিকেশন ইঞ্জিনিয়ারিং
4. Electrical Engineering (EE) — ইলেকট্রিক্যাল ইঞ্জিনিয়ারিং
5. Mechanical Engineering (ME) — মেকানিক্যাল ইঞ্জিনিয়ারিং
6. Civil Engineering (CE) — সিভিল ইঞ্জিনিয়ারিং
7. Computer Science and Design (CSD) — কম্পিউটার সায়েন্স অ্যান্ড ডিজাইন
8. CSE-AIML (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) — কম্পিউটার সায়েন্স অ্যান্ড ইঞ্জিনিয়ারিং-আর্টিফিশিয়াল ইন্টেলিজেন্স অ্যান্ড মেশিন লার্নিং
9. CSE-Data Science (DS) — কম্পিউটার সায়েন্স অ্যান্ড ইঞ্জিনিয়ারিং-ডেটা সায়েন্স
10. CSE-Cyber Security (CY) — কম্পিউটার সায়েন্স অ্যান্ড ইঞ্জিনিয়ারিং-সাইবার সিকিউরিটি

Plus MCA (এমসিএ) and MBA (এমবিএ) for postgraduate programs.

## 2. Post Graduate & Other Departments (Non-B.Tech)
- **Department of Computer Applications**: This is a separate department that offers the MCA (Master of Computer Applications) program.
- **Faculty of Management Studies (FMS)**: This is a separate department that offers the MBA (Master of Business Administration) program.

## 3. M.Tech Programs (Postgraduate Engineering)
M.Tech courses are offered by the main engineering departments. The available M.Tech specializations are in:
- Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
- Electrical Engineering (EE)
- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- Mechanical Engineering (ME)
