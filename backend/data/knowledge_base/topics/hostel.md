# BCREC Hostel Information

Dr. B.C. Roy Engineering College (BCREC) provides on-campus hostel facilities for both boys and girls.

## General Information

*   **Total Capacity**: 1500 students
*   **Total Hostels**: 5 (3 Boys' Hostels, 2 Girls' Hostels)
*   **Hostel Caution Money**: ₹ 2,000 (One-time, refundable)
*   **Hostel Allocation**: Optional (many students choose nearby PGs in the Fuljhore area to save costs)

## Room Categories & Rent

| Room Type | Rent (Per Semester) | Availability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Single Bed Room** | ₹ 30,000 | Limited (need-based allocation) |
| **Double/Triple Bed Room** | ₹ 10,000 | Standard room type |
| **4-Bed Room** | ₹ 10,000 | Maximum availability |

## Mess & Food Rules

*   **Mess Charges**: ₹ 5,000 per month (charged for 11 months per year)
*   **Meals Provided**: 4 meals per day (Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks, Dinner)
*   **Food Options**:
    *   **Veg/Non-Veg**: Non-veg (chicken, fish, or egg) is served 3-4 days per week.
    *   **Veg alternatives**: Paneer and Soybean are served daily as alternatives for vegetarians.
*   **Mess Timings**:
    *   *Breakfast*: 07:30 AM - 09:00 AM
    *   *Lunch*: 12:30 PM - 02:00 PM
    *   *Snacks*: Evening hours
    *   *Dinner*: 07:30 PM - 09:30 PM

## Curfew & Security Rules

*   **Boys' Curfew**: 08:30 PM (Winter: 07:30 PM)
*   **Girls' Curfew**: 06:30 PM (Winter: 06:00 PM)
*   **Day Guests**: Day guests are allowed on campus with prior permission from wardens. Overnight guests are strictly prohibited.
*   **Sign-in/out Policy**: Students must sign the logbook at the main gate when entering or leaving the campus after curfew.
*   **Women's Safety Helpline**: 9851006415 (available 24/7)

## Essential Items to Bring for Hostel Boarders

Students allotted a hostel room must bring their own:
1. Big and small lock (for room and wardrobe)
2. Bedding (with a mosquito net)
3. Steel glass
4. Bucket and mug
5. Torch
6. Big steel trunk (for keeping valuables locked)
