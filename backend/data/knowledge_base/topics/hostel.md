# BCREC Hostel Information

BCREC provides on-campus hostel facilities for both boys and girls. Hostel is optional.

## General Information

- **Total Capacity**: 1500 students
- **Total Hostels**: 5 (3 Boys, 2 Girls)
- **Caution Money**: Rs. 2000 (one-time, refundable)

## Room Categories & Rent

| Room Type | Rent (Per Semester) | Availability |
|-----------|--------------------|--------------|
| **Single Bed** | Rs. 30000 | Limited (need-based) |
| **Double/Triple Bed** | Rs. 10000 | Standard |
| **4-Bed** | Rs. 10000 | Maximum |

## Mess & Food

- **Monthly Charge**: Rs. 5000 per month (11 months/year)
- **Meals Per Day**: 4
- **Vegetarian**: Yes
- **Non-Veg**: Available 3-4 days/week (Chicken/Fish/Egg)
- **Veg Options**: Paneer and Soybean available daily

### Mess Timings

- **Breakfast**: 07:30 - 09:00 AM
- **Lunch**: 12:30 - 02:00 PM
- **Snacks**: Evening
- **Dinner**: 07:30 - 09:30 PM

## Curfew & Rules

- **Boys Curfew**: 08:30 PM (winter: 07:30 PM)
- **Girls Curfew**: 06:30 PM (winter: 06:00 PM)
- **Guests**: Day guests allowed with permission, no overnight stays
- **Sign-in/out**: Students must sign in/out when leaving campus after curfew
